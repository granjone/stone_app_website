Digital Wellbeing & Focus

Stone (beta)

A digital wellbeing and focus-management app that helps users reduce distractions by temporarily limiting access to selected apps and categories during scheduled focus sessions.

What Stone (beta) Does

Stone (beta) uses Apple's Screen Time technologies to let users, parents, or guardians create distraction-free periods on iPhone and iPad. During a focus session, selected apps and categories are limited so that only what is essential for work, study, or rest remains available.

The app integrates with the FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity frameworks to:

  • Request authorization to manage Screen Time settings.
  • Allow users or guardians to select which apps and categories should be restricted.
  • Apply system-level shields to the chosen apps during active focus sessions.

Privacy & Safety

Stone (beta) is designed solely for user-directed digital wellbeing and optional parental control within a Family Sharing context.

  • Screen Time–related data is processed on-device.
  • The app does not access the content of messages, emails, web pages, or personal files.
  • Data is not used for advertising, analytics, or profiling.
  • Authorization can be revoked at any time from within the app or through system settings.

Intended Use

Stone (beta) is intended for individuals and families who want to build healthier device habits, create study or work blocks, and reduce time spent in distracting apps. It is not designed or marketed for employee monitoring or any covert tracking scenario.
